## Results Overview
**1. Bar Chart**: Cakes is the  most popular sales, and tart is the lowest sales.

**2. Line Chart**: Overall sales from January to August increased despite a decline in April, but rebounded in the following months.

**3. Pie Chart**: The chart shows that Ingredients and Utilities are the two largest expenses. Other categories such as Rent, Staff, and Delivery have smaller proportions.

**4. Treemap**: Bakery and Drinks departments show the largest share, while Snacks and Frozen have smaller proportions. This type of visualization is useful for management reporting and performance review.
